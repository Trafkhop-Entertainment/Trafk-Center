Raufbold3b's Scratch Archive

Raufbold3b's Scratch Archive is a Collection of 15 very old and bad games released by HoppiTex(back then Raufbold3b/Ullidani) on Scratch/Unreleased.

History

At the time(~2018 - 2024) he did not know how to code and he was also a bit too joung to really learn it properly. So he made the best out of Scratch and other modifications like Turbowarp, Penguinmod und Dinosourmod. Most games were really bad, a few are decent but are really unfinished and bugged. In the glorious year 2025, HoppiTex made the decision to make a Collection of his "best" works(that even he disliked by now) and released that shortly after in Itch.io on 19.12.2025!
