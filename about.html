Creation

Trafkhop Entertainment™ was founded on 16.12.2025 by Raufbold3b.
It was created so that he could Publish his Games on a professional level.



What games we want to create

We want to create many things, but most importantly Videogames. We want to create a wide variety of games including many RPGs inspired from Mario & Luigi and Chrono Trigger with experimenal Game Design,
a funracer with controls mixed from Super Mario Kart/Mario Kart 8 Deluxe and new creative gameplay innovations,
a collectathon with a unique story and a cute protagonist and and party game with fun minigames and many other classic board- and cardgames.



Why we are different

We focus on a consistent and rich Lore that surpasses most others in consistancy, uniceness und logic. The lore has many formulars that ensure the consistency and lonic.
It has a complete and fully unique magic system with many formulars that makes it possible to create your own spells just with a little bit of logic.
We also focus to build in many aspects of real live physics and understanding of the universe to make it even more different from other common systems.

What we are doing right now

We are currently still in the Lore writing Phase that will take more than a year to complete. After that we will start to develop said games, unless we still have to learn the neccesary tools for said workflow.
