our magic system

We have designed a unique magic system that throws normal magic system ristrictions down the drain. It is designed almost like the german language in the sense that anyone can combine spell pieces together to creates spells, some of wich have not been seen before, not even by us! Our Level system is based on Liters, 1 Liter of Mana = 1 Level. Thats because here, Mana is measurable in volume! That also allows us to make everything consistent, because if its not, than it won't work!

our worlds and characters

Our own universe consistes of a Triverse, with one being the place of all, one being the place of none and the other the place of harmony. Our storys primarily play in the none, where 12* Realms exist, connected by rifts that form in a rather unique way. Every Realm is handcrafted and full of life and story. The 13 Realm, the Universe of harmony, is a reimagening of our own Universe, wich rerells the story of the big bang in a magical way. Every of our Characters has a full backstory, personality und apearance. There will not be 1 NPC that looks too simelar to another.
